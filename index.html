Description

Flora Corner is a cozy idle game about growing houseplants and decorating your living space. Choose from a wide variety of popular houseplants to grow, and make sure to give them their ideal light and water conditions to ewnsure they grow strong. Fully grown plants can be sold for a profit, and you can use your earnings to purchase items and furniture to decorate your room any way you want.

Features

  • Dozens of unique plant species to grow
  • Plants have unique light and water requirements
  • 100+ items and furniture pieces to decorate your room
  • Customise your furniture
